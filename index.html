How pricing data is collected:
● Metro Storage — Internal price sheet (direct)
● Access, Urban Locker, Safestore — StorageLocator.co.uk aggregator (25/50/100 sqft confirmed; 75/150 sqft interpolated)
● Big Yellow — Direct from Kings Cross quote flow (all 5 sizes, scraped daily)
● Deals — Scraped daily from competitor homepages
● Quote emails — Forms submitted weekly (Mondays) with rotating identities; inbox checked 1hr + 3hrs after

Manual Price Update

For providers where automated scraping can't extract prices (e.g. Safestore, Big Yellow), enter prices manually after getting a quote. Changes are saved to your browser and will overlay the scraped data.
